Welcome to the official website of a dynamic leader rooted in the heritage and spirit of Vizianagaram. Known for his commanding presence and lion-hearted service, Sri Nadiminti Venkata Ramana is a symbol of courage and community leadership.

About

He is a gem of personality, known for his exceptional intelligence and unwavering sincerity. With a remarkable career spanning over three decades, he served as Chief Section Supervisor in the Department of Telecom, completing an uninterrupted service of 33 years in 1999.

His dedication, leadership, and discipline have made him a role model for many, and his legacy continues to inspire generations.
